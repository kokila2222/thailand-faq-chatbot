- Thailand is a popular destination for digital nomads due to its warm climate, low cost of living, and excellent internet infrastructure.
- Visas for remote workers are limited; most use tourist visas or education visas as a workaround.
- Cities like Chiang Mai and Bangkok have strong coworking cultures and communities.
- Cost of living can range from $800 to $1,500/month depending on lifestyle.
- Opening a bank account typically requires a work permit or long-term visa.
