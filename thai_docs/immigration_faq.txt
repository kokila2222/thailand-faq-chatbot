Q: What types of visa are available for long-term stay in Thailand?
A: Non-Immigrant visa (O-A), Elite Visa, Retirement Visa, etc.

Q: What is the 90-day reporting requirement?
A: Foreigners staying over 90 days must report their address every 90 days to the Immigration Bureau.

Q: Can I extend my tourist visa?
A: Yes, you can apply for an extension at the Immigration Office, usually for 30 days.

Q: What happens if I overstay?
A: Overstaying your visa can result in fines, blacklisting, or deportation depending on the length of overstay.
